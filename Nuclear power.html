Nuclear power

Nuclear power has many benefits, although there have been events in the past that have stopped or slowed the creation of nuclear power, a sudden surge of new advancements in technology and ideas could factor in the history of our civilization. In today's time, nuclear energy might help not only quench our desire for power, but also help the future of our environment.

How it works

Nuclear power is created from splitting uranium atoms, this is called fission. When splitting uranium atoms lots of energy is released which is in the form of heat which is moved to water. The major amounts of heat created from the uranium turns the water into steam which works its way to turbines creating the power we use.

So in layman's terms:

  1. Get Uranium
  2. Enrich Uranium
  3. Put Uranium rods in Reactor Vessel
  4. Shoot electrons at it to activate the process
  5. Use the heat from the reaction to turn water into steam
  6. Spin a turbine with the steam
  7. Become rich off of selling power.

Benefits of Nuclear Energy



1 Less Pollution

Current methods of energy gathering, like Fossile Fuels are killing the environment with air pollution. Using more of or switching completely to cleaner alternatives like solar, wind, hydro, and most importantly Nuclear Power.

2 Costs Less

Although there is many ways this might seem like a more expensive alternative when accounting for enriching the fuel, getting rid of the nuclear waste, building the power plant, the amount of energy produced is actually cheaper. Gathering Fossil fuels is a more expensive way of gathering energy.

3 Base Load Energy

The amount of energy produced by the power plant can be lowered and boosted based on the amount of demand from other renewable energy sources like solar, and wind.

4 Eventually Sustainable

Although there is a fixed amount of nuclear fuel in the world, there have been ways of prolonging or getting almost an imfinite amount of energy from other sources. Breeder reactors have yet to be mastered but the idea is viable. Breeder reactors generates more fissile material then it consumes. Another way is harnessing something called Atomic Fusion. Atomic Fusion is something that happens within the sun, this creates a great amount of energy. Although Breeder reactors and Atomic Fusion havn't been solved just yet it can be done.
